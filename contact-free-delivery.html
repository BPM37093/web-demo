contact free delivery

With contact-free delivery, you can safely request your order be left at your door, in your lobby, or another safe place at your delivery location. Your driver will call or text once they drop off your food.

We will provide the option when you begin a delivery order, make sure your phone number, address, and any delivery instructions are up to date so that your driver can leave the food in the right spot.

By offering this option, we are prioritizing the health and safety of customers like you.

Based on information from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, we focused on continuing good hygiene, and using precautions when interacting with others.
